The font was created in 2020 special
for Munich Re. Font ignores
latest trends of corporative typography —
instead of this it represents precise
and optimistic aesthetics of Netherland
modernism.

Atlas also solves the problem of
retrenchment — due to tight proportions
of glyphs the company saved ink and paper.

The font is not totaly original and has it’s own geneology

This all goes to show that, while the 1950s were a
great time for grotesques, contemporary type design
has nothing to be ashamed of. Refined grown-up
designs are a sign of our blooming craft. The Atlas
collection certainly is one of them.
